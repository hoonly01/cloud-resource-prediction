The Google Cluster Trace v3 (Cell a) dataset contains multi-dimensional time-series of cloud resource utilization from Google data centers. Each instance tracks 10 metrics: cpu_usage, max_cpu_usage, mem_usage, max_mem_usage, page_cache_memory, assigned_mem, cpu_pressure, mem_pressure, cpu_burst, and mem_burst. Resource pressure metrics indicate scheduling contention when demand exceeds allocated capacity. Burst events reflect sudden demand spikes that often precede resource exhaustion and failure. The dataset is used for predicting future resource demand to support proactive scheduling and failure prevention.
